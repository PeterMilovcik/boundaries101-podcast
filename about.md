**Boundaries 101 for Men** helps men learn the skills most were never taught — how to set and maintain healthy boundaries in every area of life.

Each episode tackles a real boundary challenge men face daily — at work, in relationships, with family, and within yourself. No fluff. No shaming. Just practical, actionable coaching you can use right now.

### Topics We Cover

- **Relationships** — People-pleasing, guilt-tripping, emotional manipulation
- **Family** — Setting limits with parents, siblings, and in-laws  
- **Workplace** — Saying no to your boss, overtime boundaries, career advocacy
- **Communication** — Passive-aggressive behavior, conflict resolution, direct honesty
- **Self** — Self-respect, identity, emotional intelligence
